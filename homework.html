Ancient Egyptains used to shave their eyebrows when the family cat died. They would also throw a party where they would drink wine and beat their chests. They would then embalm the cat, and either place it in the family tomb or a pet cemetery.
One sad fact about cats: In 1888, more than 300,000 mummified cats were found in an Egyptain cemetery. They were stripped of their wrappings and carted off to be used by farmers in England and the U.S. for fertilizer.
